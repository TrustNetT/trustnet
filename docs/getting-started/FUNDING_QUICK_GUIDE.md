## FAQ: Can You Apply to All?

**Q: Can I apply to all accelerators?**  
✅ YES. Apply to 4-5 in parallel. Best practice = get 2-3 acceptances, choose the best fit.

**Q: Can I do angels + accelerator together?**  
✅ YES. Angel checks are fast (2-4 weeks), accelerator is slower (4-6 weeks to decision). Do both.

**Q: Do I have to join an accelerator?**  
❌ NO. You could: raise from angels + seed funds directly. Accelerator = faster path (mentorship + investor network).

**Q: What if I get accepted to 2 accelerators?**  
✅ GREAT PROBLEM. Choose based on:
- Mentor quality (who are the actual mentors?)
- Investor network (which fund partners attend demo days?)
- Equity % (lower equity = better)
- Fit (fintech accelerator vs blockchain accelerator)

**Q: Can I negotiate equity %?**  
⚠️ SLIGHTLY. Accelerators have standard terms (6-8% typical). Small variations possible (5-10% range). Seed funds negotiable.

**Q: What if company registration isn't approved by May?**  
✅ NO PROBLEM. You can still apply to accelerators as founder (pre-incorporation). Close seed after registration approved.

## Red Flags to Avoid

❌ **Don't say**: "We'll be the next Okta/Auth0"  
✅ **Say**: "We're building decentralized identity—Apache 2.0 open-source, cannot be acquired."

❌ **Don't say**: "We'll launch FinTechNet, HealthNet, EducationNet"  
✅ **Say**: "We add modules to ONE identity: Educational, Government, Financial modules."

❌ **Don't say**: "Our revenue comes from hosting user data"  
✅ **Say**: "Revenue from validator fees, developer tools, consulting, token appreciation."

❌ **Don't say**: "We're not yet incorporated"  
✅ **Say**: "Incorporating Q1 2026 (decision expected March 16)."
