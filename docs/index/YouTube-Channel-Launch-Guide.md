# TrustNet YouTube Channel - Complete Launch Guide

## Phase 1: Account Creation & Setup

### Step 1: Create a Google Account (if needed)
1. Go to https://accounts.google.com
2. Click "Create account"
3. Choose "For my business" → "To manage my business"
4. Fill in account details:
   - Name: TrustNet (or personal name if branding as individual)
   - Email: hello@trustnet.io (or similar branded email)
   - Password: Strong, unique password
5. Verify phone & email
6. Complete Google account setup

### Step 2: Create YouTube Channel
1. Go to https://youtube.com
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Click your profile icon (top right) → "Create a channel"
4. Enter channel name: **TrustNet**
5. Click "Create" → Confirm

### Step 3: Verify Channel (Optional but Recommended)
- Go to Creator Studio → Settings → Channel → Verify channel
- Improves eligibility for monetization & features
- Follow YouTube's verification process

## Phase 5: Video Production Workflow

### Scriptwriting
1. **Extract from content** (article/pitch notes)
2. **Structure**: Hook (15s) → Problem (30%) → Solution (50%) → CTA (5%)
3. **Tone**: Educational, confident, slightly provocative
4. **Length target**: 6-10 minutes (optimal for YouTube algorithm)

### Recording
**Tools**:
- Screen recording: OBS Studio (free) or ScreenFlow (Mac)
- Audio: Condenser mic (USB or XLR)
- Audio editing: Audacity (free) or Adobe Audition

**Setup**:
- Quiet room, soft lighting
- High-quality webcam or DSLR
- Clear, professional audio (most important)

### Editing
**Tools**:
- DaVinci Resolve (free, professional)
- Adobe Premiere Pro (subscription)
- CapCut (simple, free)

**Elements**:
- Intro/outro (2-3 seconds each)
- B-roll supporting key points
- Lower-thirds with names/titles
- Captions/subtitles (required for accessibility)
- Color grading (match TrustNet brand colors)
- Music (royalty-free from YouTube Audio Library or Epidemic Sound)

## Phase 9: Thumbnail Design

### Thumbnail Formula (Boost CTR 10-30%)
**Dimensions**: 1280×720px (16:9)
**Safe area**: Keep text/logos away from outer 50px

**Formula**: [Bold Text] + [Contrasting Image] + [Emotion] + [Logo]

### Color Scheme
- **Primary**: Teal (#00A8CC) - attention-grabbing
- **Secondary**: Dark blue (#0066CC) - contrast
- **Accent**: White or bright colors for text
- **Avoid**: Red (#FF6B35 from article covers) - less likely to click

### Text Guidelines
- Large, bold fonts (Montserrat, Arial Black)
- High contrast with background
- Max 2-3 words
- Max 2-3 lines

### Example Thumbnails
| Video | Layout |
|-------|--------|
